Ingesting CFW Logs to LTS
=========================

LTS can collect logs from CFW. It also enables real-time, efficient, and secure analysis and processing of a vast volume of collected logs.

For details, see `Configuring Logs <https://docs.otc.t-systems.com/cloud-firewall/umn/viewing_cfw_protection_logs/log_management/configuring_logs.html>`__.
